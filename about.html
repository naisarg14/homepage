About Me

I am a Bachelor of Technology student in Biotechnology at Vellore Institute of Technology, currently pursuing my Bachelor's Thesis at the University of Tartu, Estonia.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, and Multi-omics. I am passionate about developing novel computational approaches to solving complex biological problems, particularly in cancer classification and drug discovery.

I have experience with Molecular Dynamics Simulations, Molecular Docking, and various Data Science tools. I actively contribute to research publications and have led a team in the iGEM (international Genetically Engineered Machine) competition.
